Band Bio

CHVRCHES (pronounced as "churches") is a synthpop band from Glasgow Scotland that began in 2011. The band consists of Lauren Mayberry (lead vocals, drums, additional synthesisers, samplers), Iain Cook (synthesisers, guitar, bass, vocals) and Martin Doherty (synthesisers, samplers, vocals).

In 2013, CHVURCHES released their first album, "The Bones of What You Believe." In 2015, they released their second album, "Every Open Eye." They are currently working on their third album.

To listen to more songs and for more information, go to the CHVRCHES Website.
